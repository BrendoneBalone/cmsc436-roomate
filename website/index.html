Description

Running a house is a full-time job. Making sure there's food on the table, spaces are clean, and all the small tasks around the house are complete is a lot to handle. Luckily, HouseTask is here to help. HouseTask provides users with two tools to manage their households - a grocery and chores list. Each of these are shared between all members of the house, ensuring that tasks are completed and housemates are held accountable to get things done.
